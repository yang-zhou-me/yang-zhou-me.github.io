Hi, I'm a PhD student at the University of Toronto, supervised by Prof. Steven Waslander in the Toronto Robotics and AI Lab (TRAILab). Previously, I received my B.Sc. in Computer Science from Hunan University, working with Prof. Jianxin Lin.

My name is pronounced “young” and written as 洋 (“ocean”) in Chinese.

I’m broadly interested in autonomous driving, generative world models, trajectory prediction, and evaluation benchmarks that bridge vision and robotics. Always open to chat or collaborate—feel free to reach out!
